Odd John(1935)

Author: Olaf Stepledon

First edition, first issue, with ads at rear dated "535" (i.e., May, 1935); later issues bear an August date. One of 3096 copies printed. A novel that explores the idea of a Nietzschean "Super-man" with great mental prowess living among ordinary human beings. John Wainwright, the titular character, winds up feeling oppressed, and, together with fellow "Homines Superiores" he locates in mental asylums and other far-flung places, flees to an island where they form a colony. Eventually, the activities of the colonists are looked upon by the rest of humanity with suspicion, and when the island is invaded, the colonists deliberately destroy it.
